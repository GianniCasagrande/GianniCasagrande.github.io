Axonify Content UI

UX Research • Visual Design • Prototyping • Front End Development

{image}

About

The Axonify content management system is one of the core features of the platform. Users come here to create and manage all the microlearning content they want to serve their employees. Content can include different question types (multiple choice, multi-answer, matching, fill in the blank), SCORM modules, Video training, Surveys and Key performance indicators.

{image}

Problem

The old iteration of content was showing its age as it was a very click intensive bulky UX. The old iteration was also not supporting industry training design best practices around strategic planning, key learning points, change audits and reviewing.

Constraints

Had to respect the current content hierarchy: Category > Subject > Topic Still needed to support a “click to edit and click to save” paradigm. Autosave was to large of a change from a server development point of view, especially for a v1.

{image}

High-Level Goals

  • Streamlined content creation, editing and publishing
  • Bake in industry standards: training plan, key learning points
  • Integrated review process, including off platform reviews
  • Import & Export of content
  • Audit Trail
  • Easy content retrieval and searching

Research

  • Client interviews, how were they creating content? Where they using industry standards?
  • Detailed persona creation with the PM team
  • Internal Instructional design interviews with Axonify Instructional design teams
  • Show me how the process works today
  • How do key learning points (KLPs) link to content and difficulty levels
  • How do you currently collaborating with customers
  • Is there anything missing from your current process?
  • Iterative design with ID and PM teams
  • UI prototype testing with internal and external stakeholders
{image}

Solution

For the new content UI I opted for a 2 panel “live” view. Essentially each level would have details view with the ability to drill into a sub-view of that category. All editing and creation would be done in these views as well.

Key Features

  • One click editing
  • Easy Creation
  • Bulk Operations
  • Searching and Filtering of Content
  • Commenting and Auditing
  • Content review with review assignment
  • 19+ language support
